Our Services

We specialise in interior-focused upgrades designed to enhance comfort, aesthetics, and technology while maintaining the integrity of your vehicle.

Ambient Lighting

Custom-installed multi-colour ambient lighting systems seamlessly integrated into doors, dashboards, footwells and centre consoles. App-controlled with brightness and colour customisation.

Starlight Roofs

Premium fibre-optic starlight headliners with custom layouts, shooting star effects, and brightness control. A luxury upgrade inspired by high-end vehicles.

CarPlay & Android Auto

Infotainment upgrades bringing modern Apple CarPlay and Android Auto functionality to vehicles that did not originally come equipped.

Interior Enhancements

Additional interior upgrades including trim enhancements, lighting accents, screen upgrades, and tailored interior customisation.
